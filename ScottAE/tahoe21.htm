SCOTT'S AT TAHOE-DONNER


REQUESTS  OF  OUR  GUESTS
 No pets (dogs or cats)
 Maximum occupancy is 6 people
 No large parties
 Water shut-off valve under downstair bathroom sink by turning T bar 90 degress clockwise
 In winter (when snow or wet outside) outdoor shoes only worn in the entryway and downstairs bathroom
 Bedding & linens are in the upstair closet (outside bathroom)
 Bath towels and hand towels are in downstairs bathroom cabinet
 
MORNING OF DEPARTURE
 Strip bedding and place linens in laundry room hamper.
 Place bathroom towels in laundry room hamper.
 Run dishwasher 2 hours prior to departure.
 Leave any guest cards used for Tahoe Donner facilities on the kitchen counter.
 
UPON  DEPARTURE
 Turn water OFF by turning T bar under downstair bathroom sink 90 degress counterclockwise
 After turning water off, open kitchen water faucet to bleed water lines
 Set Heater to 50 degrees
 Turn off stereo, TV, and subwoofer
 Make sure dishwasher, washer, oven, and all other appliances are off
 Close all lock all windows
 Close all curtains and blinds
 Turn off all lights
 Lock and deadbolt front and back doors (backdoor lock broken, please use deadbolt
 Lock garage doors (if open)
 Please take trash with you. We do not have trash service. Animals will break into garbage locker.
 
 WE HOPE YOU ENJOYED YOUR STAY !
