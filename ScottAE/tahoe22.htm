HOW TO USE ALL THOSE DARN CONTROLLERS !
 TV CONTROLLER - PHILLIPS / MAGNAVOX
    - Power - red button top right
    - Volume Up/Down - bottom left (can also use tuner - see below)
    - Channel Up/Down - bottom right
 RADIO / TUNER - SONY CONTOLLER
   - Power - green button upper right
   - Tuner - white tuner button - upper right
   - Volume - bottom right
 TV WITH SURROUND SOUND
   - TV Controller - see TV controller described above
   - Sony Controller - press DVD/LD - white button upper middle
   - Sony Controller - volume lower right
 DVD PLAYER - RCA CONTROLLER
    - Set TV to EXT1 (not SVHS) TV channels start at EXT1, SVHS, 1, 2, 3, 4.....
    - Power on DVD player - red button on top row of RCA controller
    - Load DVD in RCA unit (see eject button on front of unit).
    - Press blue PLAY button in bottom middle of RCA controller
    - When menu appears, press OK button in middle of RCA controller
      (some DVDs vary... you may need to switch pressing PLAY and OK buttons)
 VHS PLAYER - TOSHIBA CONTROLLER
   - Set TV to channel 4
   - Power on VHS player from Toshiba controller
   - Load VHS tape
   - Press PLAY button in bottom middle of Toshiba controller
      (button is white and labeled CURSOR)
 SUBWOOFER - UNDER COFFEE / END TABLE
   - There are two knobs and two switches
   - Top knob is volume
   - Power is top switch (3 position switch)
     (far left position is off, middle is on, far right is on & 180 phase shift)
